Fish Tacos with Mango Salsa

Ingredients

  • 200g white fish fillets (tilapia, basa, or cod)
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 4 small tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage or lettuce

    • For Mango Salsa

  • 1 ripe mango (diced)
  • 1/4 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chilli or jalapeño (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp lemon or lime juice
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Season fish with chilli powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the fish on medium heat for 3–4 minutes per side until done. Flake it gently.
  3. In a bowl, mix mango, onion, chilli, lemon juice, coriander, and salt to make salsa.
  4. Warm tortillas lightly.
  5. Place cabbage or lettuce on each tortilla, add flaked fish, and top with mango salsa.
  6. Serve immediately with a squeeze of lime!
