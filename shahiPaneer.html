Shahi Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp cashew paste or 8–10 cashews (soaked and blended)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes away.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, salt, and a pinch of sugar. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in cashew paste and mix well.
  5. Add cream and garam masala. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and gently coat in the gravy. Simmer for another 3–4 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve with naan or rice.
