Chicken Alfredo Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 200g cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
  • 6 lasagna sheets
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (mozzarella or cheddar)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder or minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed herbs or oregano (optional)
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna sheets as per packet instructions. Drain and keep aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add flour. Stir for a minute to make a roux.
  3. Gradually add milk, whisking to avoid lumps. Cook until slightly thickened.
  4. Add cream, garlic, cheese, salt, pepper, and herbs. Mix until smooth to form Alfredo sauce.
  5. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a baking dish.
  6. Layer: Alfredo sauce, lasagna sheet, chicken, more sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with extra cheese and bake for 20–25 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let it rest for 5 minutes, garnish with parsley, and serve warm!
