Dhokla

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp Eno fruit salt or baking soda
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • For Tempering:
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp water

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, yogurt, ginger-green chili paste, turmeric, sugar, lemon juice, and salt to form a smooth batter.
  2. Let the batter rest for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Just before steaming, add Eno fruit salt or baking soda and mix gently until batter becomes frothy.
  4. Pour batter into a greased steaming tray.
  5. Steam for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Heat oil for tempering, add mustard seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves.
  7. Pour tempering over the steamed dhokla.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve warm with chutney.
