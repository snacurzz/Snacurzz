Rajma Chawal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma (kidney beans), soaked overnight
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 cups cooked basmati rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Pressure cook soaked rajma with 3 cups water for 5–6 whistles or until soft.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till they soften.
  6. Add turmeric, chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add cooked rajma with water. Mash a few beans for thicker gravy.
  8. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until it turns thick and flavorful.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  10. Serve hot with steamed basmati rice!
