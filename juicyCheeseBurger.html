Juicy Cheese Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 burger bun (sliced)
  • 1 beef or veggie patty
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices onion
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Butter for toasting
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the burger buns with butter on a pan until golden brown.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patty until cooked through. Add cheese slices on top to melt.
  3. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on the inside of both buns.
  4. Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, and the cheesy patty.
  5. Close the burger, press gently, and serve hot!
