Falafel Pita Pocket

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 3–4 garlic cloves
  • Fresh coriander or parsley (a handful)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 4 pita breads
  • Sliced cucumber, tomato, and lettuce
  • Yogurt or tahini sauce (for dressing)

Steps

  1. Drain soaked chickpeas and blend with onion, garlic, coriander, spices, baking soda, salt, and pepper into a coarse paste.
  2. Refrigerate the mixture for 30 minutes. Then form small balls or patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry or shallow-fry falafels until golden brown.
  4. Warm pita breads and slice open to make pockets.
  5. Fill each pita with lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and 2–3 falafel pieces.
  6. Drizzle with yogurt or tahini sauce and serve immediately!
