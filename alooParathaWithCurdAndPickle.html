Aloo Paratha with Curd & Pickle

Ingredients

  • For Dough:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water as needed
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil (optional)
  • For Filling:
  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled & mashed
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango) powder or lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • For Serving:
  • 1/2 cup plain curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp pickle (any variety you love)

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, and water to knead a soft dough. Cover and rest it for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix all filling ingredients: mashed potatoes, chilies, ginger, spices, coriander, and salt.
  3. Divide dough into equal balls. Roll one ball into a small disc.
  4. Place 2 tbsp of filling in the center, gather the edges, and seal it gently.
  5. Roll it out again carefully into a medium-thick paratha.
  6. Heat a tawa or pan and cook the paratha on medium heat, flipping until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Apply ghee or butter on both sides and press lightly to cook evenly.
  8. Serve hot with chilled curd and your favorite pickle on the side.
