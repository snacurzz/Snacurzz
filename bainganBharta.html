Baingan Bharta

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants (baingan)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Juice of half a lemon

Steps

  1. Roast eggplants directly on gas flame or in the oven till the skin chars and flesh becomes soft.
  2. Cool, peel off the charred skin, and mash the pulp roughly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan; add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and sauté till golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt; cook till tomatoes soften.
  7. Add mashed eggplant and mix well.
  8. Cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Turn off heat, add lemon juice and coriander leaves.
  10. Mix well and serve hot with roti or naan.
