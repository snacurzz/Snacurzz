Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 4 pav buns
  • Lemon wedges & butter for serving

Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan, sauté onions till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook till soft and mushy.
  4. Add capsicum, mashed potatoes, peas, salt, chili powder & pav bhaji masala.
  5. Mash everything well using a potato masher or the back of a spoon.
  6. Simmer for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add water if needed for consistency.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and a small cube of butter on top.
  8. Toast pav buns in butter until golden brown.
  9. Serve hot with lemon wedges and the buttery bhaji!
