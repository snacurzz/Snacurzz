Chole Bhature

Ingredients

  • For Chole (Chickpea Curry):
  • 1 cup chickpeas (kabuli chana), soaked overnight
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp chole masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • For Bhature:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder or baking soda
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with water until soft (about 6-7 whistles).
  2. Heat oil in a pan; add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
  3. Add onions and sauté till golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, chole masala, and salt.
  6. Cook until oil separates from the masala.
  7. Add cooked chickpeas with some cooking water, simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  9. For bhature dough, mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  10. Add water gradually and knead into soft dough.
  11. Cover and rest for 2 hours.
  12. Divide dough into balls and roll into circles.
  13. Heat oil in a deep pan; deep fry each bhatura till puffed and golden.
  14. Drain excess oil and serve hot with chole.
