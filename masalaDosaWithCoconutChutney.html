Masala Dosa with Coconut Chutney

Ingredients

  • For Dosa Batter:
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/3 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • For Potato Filling:
  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • For Coconut Chutney:
  • 1 cup grated fresh coconut
  • 2 tbsp roasted chana dal (Bengal gram)
  • 1-2 green chilies
  • 1-inch piece ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds (for tempering)
  • 5-6 curry leaves (for tempering)
  • 1 dried red chili (for tempering)

Steps

  1. Prepare Dosa Batter:
  2. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6-8 hours.
  3. Grind urad dal and fenugreek to a smooth batter, then grind rice coarsely.
  4. Mix both batters, add salt, and ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  5. Make Potato Filling:
  6. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, let them splutter.
  7. Add onions, green chilies, curry leaves; sauté till onions turn translucent.
  8. Add turmeric and salt.
  9. Add mashed potatoes, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and set aside.
  11. Make Coconut Chutney:
  12. Grind coconut, roasted chana dal, green chilies, ginger, salt, and water to smooth consistency.
  13. In a small pan, heat oil for tempering. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dried red chili.
  14. Pour tempering over the chutney and mix.
  15. Cook Dosa:
  16. Heat a non-stick pan or cast iron griddle.
  17. Pour a ladle of batter and spread thin in a circular motion.
  18. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around edges; cook till golden and crisp.
  19. Place a spoonful of potato filling on one side and fold dosa over it.
  20. Serve hot with coconut chutney!
