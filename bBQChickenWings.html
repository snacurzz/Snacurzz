BBQ Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken wings
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional, for extra glaze)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (or use air fryer or pan method).
  2. Season chicken wings with salt, pepper, paprika, and oil. Mix well.
  3. Arrange on a baking tray and bake for 25–30 minutes or until crispy and cooked.
  4. In a bowl, mix BBQ sauce and honey if using.
  5. Toss baked wings in the BBQ sauce to coat evenly.
  6. Optional: Bake again for 5 minutes for stickier texture.
  7. Serve hot with dip or salad on the side!
