Paneer Tikka Wrap

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Sliced onions, capsicum, and tomatoes
  • 2–3 whole wheat or maida wraps/rotis
  • Green chutney or mayo (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix curd, besan, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chilli powder, garam masala, salt, and oil in a bowl.
  2. Add paneer cubes and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan or grill and cook marinated paneer with sliced veggies until slightly charred.
  4. Warm the wraps or rotis on a pan.
  5. Spread green chutney or mayo if using.
  6. Add the paneer tikka and veggies mixture in the center of each wrap.
  7. Roll tightly, wrap in foil or parchment if needed, and serve hot!
