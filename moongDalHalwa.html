Moong Dal Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal (split yellow gram)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup full-fat milk
  • 3/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 cashew nuts, chopped
  • 10-12 almonds, chopped
  • Raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse moong dal and soak for 2 hours. Drain well.
  2. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan; add moong dal and roast on medium heat until golden brown and aromatic, stirring continuously.
  3. In a separate pot, boil water and milk together.
  4. Slowly add the boiling liquid to the roasted dal in batches, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  5. Cook the mixture on low heat, stirring often until the dal absorbs all the liquid and becomes thick.
  6. Add sugar and cardamom powder; cook while stirring until the sugar dissolves and the mixture thickens again.
  7. In a small pan, fry cashew nuts, almonds, and raisins in a little ghee and add them to the halwa.
  8. Cook for a few more minutes until the halwa starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  9. Serve warm, garnished with more nuts if desired.
