Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer, cut into cubes
  • 4 cups fresh spinach leaves (palak), washed and chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream or 2 tbsp yogurt (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer to cold water.
  2. Drain and blend spinach to a smooth puree.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add onions and sauté till golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell disappears.
  6. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt; cook till oil separates.
  7. Add spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.
  9. Add garam masala and cream/yogurt; simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Turn off heat and add butter if using.
  11. Serve hot with roti or naan!
