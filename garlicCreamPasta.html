Garlic Cream Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cream or milk
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • Mixed vegetables (capsicum, corn, carrots, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chilli flakes and oregano (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic and onion until soft.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Pour in cream (or milk) and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add cheese if using, stir until smooth and creamy.
  6. Add pasta, toss well to coat evenly.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and optional herbs. Serve hot!
