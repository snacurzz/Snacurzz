Veg Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil (coconut or vegetable)
  • 2 tbsp Thai green curry paste
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water or vegetable stock
  • Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, baby corn, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1 cup jasmine rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook jasmine rice as per package instructions. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the green curry paste for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add coconut milk and water. Mix well and bring to a gentle boil.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and cook until they’re tender but not mushy.
  5. Add soy sauce, sugar (if using), and salt to taste. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat and add fresh basil leaves if available.
  7. Serve hot with jasmine rice.
