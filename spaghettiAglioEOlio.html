Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4–5 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/4 cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté sliced garlic until golden (not burnt).
  3. Add chilli flakes and stir for a few seconds.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti and toss well.
  5. Pour in reserved pasta water to loosen up if needed.
  6. Mix in chopped parsley and check seasoning.
  7. Top with parmesan if using. Serve hot!
