Litti Chokha

Ingredients

  • For Litti Dough:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • Water (as needed)
  • For Stuffing (Sattu Mixture):
  • 1 cup roasted gram flour (sattu)
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • For Chokha:
  • 2 medium roasted eggplants
  • 2 medium boiled potatoes
  • 2 tomatoes, roasted
  • 2 green chilies
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt and lemon juice to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, and mustard oil; knead into a firm dough with water.
  2. For stuffing, combine sattu, mustard oil, garlic, ginger, green chilies, lemon juice, ajwain, salt, and coriander.
  3. Divide dough into small balls, stuff each with sattu mixture, and seal properly.
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) or prepare a traditional coal fire.
  5. Bake or roast litti until golden and cooked through, about 25-30 minutes.
  6. For chokha, mash roasted eggplants, boiled potatoes, and roasted tomatoes together.
  7. Add chopped green chilies, mustard oil, salt, lemon juice, and coriander; mix well.
  8. Serve hot litti with chokha and extra mustard oil.
