Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

At Snacurzz, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our website.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or request any personal information from our users. There is no sign-up, login, or user account system on our website.

2. No User Tracking

Snacurzz does not use any user-tracking systems or cookies to identify or monitor visitors. Your visit is anonymous, and we do not save any data from you.

3. Third-party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may use third-party advertising services like Google AdSense to show ads on our website. These services may use cookies to display relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

You can learn how Google uses your data by visiting Google's Advertising Policy.

4. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites.

5. Children's Privacy

Snacurzz is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with the updated date mentioned above.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, feel free to contact us.
