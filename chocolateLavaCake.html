Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder (for greasing ramekins)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (428°F). Grease ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together until smooth. Let it cool slightly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale.
  4. Fold the melted chocolate into the egg mixture gently.
  5. Sift flour over the mixture and fold again until smooth.
  6. Pour into ramekins and bake for 10–12 minutes. Edges should be firm, center soft.
  7. Let them cool for a minute, then invert onto plates and serve immediately.
