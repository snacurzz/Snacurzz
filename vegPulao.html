Veg Pulao

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes), chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse basmati rice until water runs clear; soak for 20 minutes and drain.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a pan, add bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and cumin seeds; sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and salt; cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add drained rice and gently sauté for 2 minutes.
  7. Add water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  8. Cook until rice is done and water is absorbed (about 15 minutes).
  9. Fluff rice gently, garnish with coriander leaves, and serve hot.
