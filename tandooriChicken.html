Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (preferably leg quarters)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin powder
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil or any cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Red food color (optional, for authentic look)
  • Fresh coriander and lemon wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. Clean and make slits in the chicken pieces for better marination.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, mustard oil, salt, and red food color (if using).
  3. Coat the chicken pieces thoroughly with the marinade.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  5. Preheat grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
  6. Grill or bake the chicken pieces for 20-25 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and slightly charred.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and lemon wedges before serving.
