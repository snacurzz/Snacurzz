Pork Curry with Rice

Ingredients

  • For Pork Curry:
  • 500g pork (preferably with some fat), cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • For Steamed Rice:
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse pork pieces and set aside. Rinse rice and soak for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy pan. Add chopped onions and sauté till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook till the raw smell goes away.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till soft and oil separates.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander, and cumin powders. Mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add pork cubes and salt. Sauté on high heat for 4-5 minutes until the pork is sealed.
  7. Add water, cover, and simmer on low heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour until pork is tender.
  8. Finish with garam masala and chopped coriander leaves. Let it rest covered for 5 minutes.
  9. Meanwhile, cook rice: boil water with salt, add soaked rice, cover and cook on low till done.
  10. Serve hot pork curry with fluffy steamed rice for a hearty meal.
