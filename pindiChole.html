Pindi Chole

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chole masala (Punjabi spice mix)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur powder (dry mango powder)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tsp baking soda (optional, for softening chickpeas)

Steps

  1. Drain soaked chickpeas and pressure cook with enough water and baking soda (if using) until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and dried red chilies, sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, sauté until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chole masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder; cook spices for a minute.
  5. Add cooked chickpeas along with cooking water; mix well.
  6. Simmer uncovered on medium heat until the gravy thickens.
  7. Add amchur powder and garam masala; adjust salt and cook for 5 more minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with bhature or rice.
