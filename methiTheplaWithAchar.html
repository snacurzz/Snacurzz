Methi Thepla with Achar

Ingredients

  • For Thepla Dough:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup fresh fenugreek leaves (methi), finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • For Achar (Pickle):
  • Store-bought or homemade Indian mango or lime pickle

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients for the thepla dough in a bowl; add water gradually to make a soft dough.
  2. Cover and rest the dough for 15–20 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into small balls and roll each into thin circles.
  4. Heat a tawa (griddle) and cook the theplas on both sides, applying a little oil until golden brown spots appear.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite Indian achar (pickle) on the side.
