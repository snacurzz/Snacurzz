Besan Cheela

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to make batter)
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, turmeric, chili powder, cumin seeds, and salt.
  2. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and coriander.
  3. Add water gradually to form a smooth, pourable batter.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa, grease lightly with oil.
  5. Pour a ladle of batter and spread into a thin circle.
  6. Cook on medium heat until edges start to lift and bottom turns golden.
  7. Flip and cook the other side till golden and cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
